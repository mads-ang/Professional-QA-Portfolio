# Salesforce Data Integrity & Validation
**Objective:** To ensure that the Salesforce CRM maintains high data quality and adheres to business rules during the lead-to-opportunity conversion process.

### Focus Areas:

* **Validation Rules:** Preventing "dirty data" from entering the pipeline.
* **Business Logic:** Ensuring mandatory fields are populated before a record transitions.
* **Data Accuracy:** Maintaining consistency for downstream sales reporting.
